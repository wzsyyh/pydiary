「学习笔记」决策单调性dp/斜率优化dp

Author: Push_Y's blog Date: 2021-06-30 Reading Time:3.3 mins words:578 Views: -

听周神讲完，对此有些新的理解吧

证明决策单调性，不会四边形不等式也没关系

先以经典题P2900 [USACO08MAR]Land Acquisition G/土地购买为例。

f[i]=min⁡{f[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i]}f[i]=\min\{f[j]+y[j+1]*x[i]\} f[i]=min{f[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i]}

用 g[i]g[i]g[i] 表示转移到 f[i]f[i]f[i] 最优的决策点，证明 g[i]<=g[i+1]g[i]<=g[i+1]g[i]<=g[i+1] ：

根据 g[i]g[i]g[i] 的定义，相当于已知了（下面 jjj 为任意决策点）

f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]∗x[i]≤[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i]f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]*x[i]≤[j]+y[j+1]*x[i] f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]∗x[i]≤[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i]

那等价于证明：

f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]∗x[i+1]≤f[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i+1]    (j≤g[i])f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]*x[i+1]≤f[j]+y[j+1]*x[i+1]\ \ \ \ (j≤g[i]) f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]∗x[i+1]≤f[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i+1]    (j≤g[i])

利用已知：

f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]∗x[i+1]+y[g[i]+1]∗(x[i]−x[i+1])≤f[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i+1]+y[j+1]∗(x[i]−x[i+1])f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]*x[i+1]+y[g[i]+1]*(x[i]-x[i+1])≤f[j]+y[j+1]*x[i+1]+y[j+1]*(x[i]-x[i+1])f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]∗x[i+1]+y[g[i]+1]∗(x[i]−x[i+1])≤f[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i+1]+y[j+1]∗(x[i]−x[i+1])

∵j≤g[i] ∴y[g[i]+1]≤y[j+1]∵j≤g[i]\ ∴y[g[i]+1]≤y[j+1]∵j≤g[i] ∴y[g[i]+1]≤y[j+1]

∵x[i]<x[i+1] ∴x[i]−x[i+1]<0∵x[i]<x[i+1]\ ∴x[i]-x[i+1]<0∵x[i]<x[i+1] ∴x[i]−x[i+1]<0

∴y[g[i]+1]∗(x[i]−x[i+1])≥y[j+1]∗(x[i]−x[i+1])∴y[g[i]+1]*(x[i]-x[i+1])≥y[j+1]*(x[i]-x[i+1])∴y[g[i]+1]∗(x[i]−x[i+1])≥y[j+1]∗(x[i]−x[i+1])

∴f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]∗x[i]<=f[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i]∴f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]*x[i]<=f[j]+y[j+1]*x[i]∴f[g[i]]+y[g[i]+1]∗x[i]<=f[j]+y[j+1]∗x[i]
